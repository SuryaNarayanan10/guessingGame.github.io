How to play?
  • Computer will think of a secret four-letter word.
  • You have to makes a guess by writing a four-letter word.
  • For each guessed letter that is in the correct position, it's considered a "bull."
  • For each guessed letter that is in the secret word but in the wrong position, it's considered a "cow."
  • You have to make continues guesses, and after each guess, the feedback (cows and bulls) is provided until the correct word is guessed.
  • Click to strike out unwanted letters in previously guessed words and also use rough dashes for guessing positions.
  • If you correctly guess the secret word (4 bulls), you WIN :).
